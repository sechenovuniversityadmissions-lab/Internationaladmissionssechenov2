How to Apply?

Step 1. Choose the program;

Step 2. Submit the necessary documents via ruID, by post, or in-person;

Step 3. Take the entrance exams and score at least the minimal passing points;

Step 4. Request a contract and get it in your applicant account (in the case of a paid-based training);

Step 5. Sign the contract and pay for the training (if applicable); 

Step 6. Congratulations! You're in, so now you may prepare your travel to Moscow, and contact the migration department in case you need a visa.

New entry rules to Russia: 

  • Citizens of visa-free countries – submit an application for entry in advance (3-90 days before entry) via the ruID application and receive positive response.

  • All foreigners who have not previously confirmed biometrics in Russia should visit a bank within 10 days of arrival. You will not be able to obtain a SIM card or access government services without this confirmation.

FAQ

May I get a scholarship?

There're only government scholarships, the university itself doesn't provide scholarships (grants, discounts, etc.).

There are only 2 ways to get a scholarship: 

1. To get a scholarship (so-called quota) from the Ministry of Science and Higher Education that covers tuition fees as well. 

Apply for quota of the Ministry of Science and Higher Education through the link. 

2. To win the International Olympiad "Open Doors: Russian Scholarship project Open Doors: Russian Scholarship project (Master and PhD programs). 

In case you win, you'll get a scholarship for the next academic year.

NB: The university doesn't provide recommendation letters, primary acceptances etc. for scholarship applications.


How do I transfer from my university to Sechenov University?

Transfers may only be accepted from other Russian universities, it's not possible to transfer from abroad. For any further questions concerning your transfer, please contact perevod@student.sechenov.ru.

NB: You can't transfer your scholarship to Sechenov.


When does the application process begin?

Admissions usually begin in June, and close by September. In case the admissions' period is extended, the announcement on it is published at the university website.


How do I apply for admission to Sechenov University?

You may apply via ruID, by post, or in-person.


How long do I have to wait for a visa invitation?

An invitation for visa may only be issued after your enrollment in the university. You'll need to contact the migration department invitation@staff.sechenov.ru then and submit the required documents. Once the migration department accepts your documents, it will take them 21 working days to get you an invitation.


I want to enter SU, what do I have to do?

You should choose a program, check the documents required, then apply timely following the steps mentioned above.


Why am i required to travel to an HCT Country if i am a USA OR Canada citizen??

Due to the ongoing geopolitical and medical emergencies Russian colleges are Required to accept USA and Canada issued documetns only if they are Notarized and transalted from an HCT country Such as China , India and Kazakhstan. These countries take different amount of time to process your documents, So applicants are advised to travel accordingly. For inquiry about the travel plans please email us at International Sechenov Admissons


What level of Russian language proficiency is required for admission to Sechenov University?

There's an entrance exam in Russian for undergraduate programs.

No language certificate (IELTS or other) is required.


How can i get a study visa for the entrance test as a US/Canada ctiizen for 2026-27 session?

We regret to inform you that, due to current geopolitical circumstances, U.S. and Canadian citizens may be unable to obtain student visas from their home countries in time. Candidates are advised to complete their documentation in an HCT country and apply for a short-term student visa. Based on current information, visa processing is fastest in India (approximately 1 week) and China (approximately 10 days). Please ensure you remain on schedule for your session. We apologize for any inconvenience and appreciate your understanding.


What documents do I have to submit in order to be admitted to Sechenov University?

The following original documents are required to be provided to the Admission Committee:

1. Your passport and its in-person notarized translation into Russian from an HCT country;

2. Your education certificate should have consular legalization mark or be bearing the Apostille or e-Apostille stamp.For this admission session we are waiving the need of traditional "Apostille" for an "e-Apsotille" required the submission of travel information before 31st june. Please note, education certificates obtained in the Russian Federation do not require legalization, Apostille stamps or Certificates of Recognition.

3. An in-person notarized translation of the education certificate into Russian From an HCT country. 

4. The Certificate of Recognition of foreign education issued from an HCT country. It is provided by the National Accreditation Agency.

All documents issued outside Russia must be legalized prior to submission in accordance with the law of the Russian Federation: undergo consular legalization (in the Consulate of the Russian Federation in the country where the document was issued) or bear an "Apostille" or an "e-Apostille" stamp (for documents issued in the countries that are parties to The Hague Convention) And be translated and notarized from an HCT country. Original documents rather than copies should be legalized! 
Submission of travel documents, proof of an emergency and notarization appoinment ID from respective country befoe 31st June are a must for the waiver of a traditional Apostille and use of an e-Apostille.


Does Sechenov University provide a dormitory?

Sechenov University provides on-campus housing in 5 dormitories and additional living spaces through partner facilities.


Do I need a health insurance?

Yes, health insurance is a must, you can find the list of insurance companies that meet University requirements below:

1) Reso;
2) ICICI Lombard-India;
3) HDFC Ergo;
4) Lexgarant;
5) Rosgosstrah.

!!! Information for Chinese Applicants:

Sechenov University, XiPei Education, and Unibase Russia.Study have opened a testing сenter in Hangzhou (China)

Sechenov University's foreign office has commenced a new phase with the establishment of this testing facility. This initiative is designed to enhance the selection process for Chinese applicants, ensuring that only the most capable and well-prepared candidates are admitted to Sechenov University.

How does it work?

The testing centre in Hangzhou has been designed to meet international standards. It facilitates a transparent and objective examination process, with the capacity to accommodate up to 1,056 candidates concurrently.

Why is this important?

  • Honest selection is an admission opportunity for truly prepared students.

  • Convenience: Prospective applicants can now undertake examinations within China, eliminating the need for time-consuming travel to Russia.

  • Reduction of bureaucratic complexities: the admission process becomes easier and more accessible.

  • Saving time and money: students and their families avoid unnecessary travel and living expenses.

Advancing International Collaboration

The launch of the Hangzhou testing centre marks a logical progression in the ongoing efforts of Sechenov University’s foreign office in China. This development not only simplifies the admissions process but also reinforces the institution’s commitment to attracting top-tier Chinese candidates.

Sechenov University, alongside XiPei Education and Unibase Russia.Study, remains dedicated to creating optimal conditions for international admissions, with this project representing another important step forward in the evolution of world-class medical education.
